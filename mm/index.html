How to play

  • Tap a cell to focus it; tap again to flip across/down.
  • Type letters to fill.
  • Backspace clears the current cell or steps back.
  • ← → ↑ ↓ move the cursor.
  • Space switches between across and down.
  • Tab / Shift+Tab jump to next / previous word.
